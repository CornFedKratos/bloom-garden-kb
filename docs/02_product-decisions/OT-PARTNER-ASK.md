# Bloom Garden — Founding Clinical Partner Invitation

*A clinical collaboration proposal for pediatric occupational therapists*

---

## What We Are Building

Bloom Garden is a HIPAA-compliant emotional regulation platform for autistic children ages 4–12 — connecting them, their parents, and their occupational therapists as a continuous care system. It is built by a parent of two neurodivergent children, with occupational therapy as the clinical foundation.

The governing principle: *"We help children. We do not seek validation for ourselves."* Everything in this product — every design decision, every mechanic, every data point — exists to serve the child.

---

## Why We Are Coming To You First

We built the clinical governance before we wrote a single line of code. Every mechanic in Bloom Garden must pass through two gates before it ships:

- **Phase 1 (Research Gate):** Peer-reviewed evidence confirmed before any mechanic enters the spec
- **Phase 2 (Clinical Gate):** An OT observes the mechanic with a real child before it is approved to ship

Founding clinical partners are not reviewers. They are co-builders. Your observation is the gate that determines what gets built — before it is built at scale.

---

## What We Are Asking

The minimum commitment is approximately two hours:

| Activity | Time |
|---|---|
| Read four clinical evidence briefs | ~30 minutes |
| Observe one child using the prototype | Up to 60 minutes (child-led) |
| Debrief conversation | ~30 minutes |

If you choose to continue beyond this initial commitment, there are two optional paths:
- Bi-weekly feedback sessions during the closed pilot phase
- Co-authorship of the clinical exercise library (credited in the product)

---

## What You Receive

- Permanent free access to Bloom Garden for your practice — all subscription tiers, for as long as the product exists
- Named founding clinical partner credit in the product and knowledge base — your clinical input is documented as part of the product's history
- Co-authorship credit on any published exercise library materials
- First visibility into the product before any public launch
- The ability to shape the tool your patients will use — before it is finished, not after

---

## What We Will Not Ask

- No public endorsement or promotion
- No validation of clinical claims we have not earned
- No time beyond what is described above unless you choose to continue
- No compromise of your clinical judgment — you may observe a mechanic and determine it is contraindicated. That is the right outcome if it is true.

---

## The Clinical Evidence Basis

The four mechanics in this prototype each have a documented evidence base:

- **Co-regulation theory** — companion-based regulation support (CEB-001)
- **Projective play** — child-directed symbolic play as regulation vehicle (CEB-002)
- **Diaphragmatic breathing** — visual-led breathing support for interoceptive differences (CEB-003)
- **Proprioceptive awareness support** — sensory-informed engagement mechanics (CEB-004)

Each brief includes the peer-reviewed evidence, what is clearly established, what is extrapolated and why, and the specific questions your observation will answer. They are provided with the prototype.
